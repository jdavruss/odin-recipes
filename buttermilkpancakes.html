Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Mix together the four, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl mix the eggs, butter milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry.
  4. Heat non-stick pan on medium heat.
  5. Drop batter onto non-stick pan.
  6. Once bubbles begin to form flip the pancake
  7. Serve with choice of topping, such as maple syrup or fruit.
  8. ENJOY!
