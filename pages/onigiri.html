Onigiri

Ingredients

  • Japanese Short-Grain Rice
  • Nori Seaweed
  • Optional Fillings

Directions

  1. Cook short-grain rice with rice cooker, or on the stove
  2. Using a mold, or your hands, make rice balls. Don't forget to salt the rice balls and add your chosen filling.
  3. Wrap the rice ball with nori seaweed.
